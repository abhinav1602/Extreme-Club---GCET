ABOUT US

"Lets go invent tomorrow instead of worrying about what happened yesterday".

Web Development

The web development Club is intended for new, intermediate, and advanced web developers/designers. Our goal is to build a strong foundation for web development/design community for the students.

Technical

The technical club aims to create awareness of various technical happenings around the world among the students and encourages them to actively participate in related events.

Editorial

The editorial club has been created with the purpose of providing a medium for enhancing the writing and presenting skills of students.

Photography

The Photography Club's objective is to encourage members in their photographic endeavours, and provide opportunities to improve their skills through various competitions.

Sports

The philosophy of the Sport Club is to promote health, physical well being and the acquisition of physical skill development of the students.

Cultural

The cultural club aims to encourage student’s interest, participation, and responsibility in the ingenious field through a medium of creative art and literary curriculum & Scope. The inventiveness is to provide social, cultural and recreational activities for the college community.
